Support

FAQs

Do you have questions about our courses? Our FAQ may help you quicker!

Are there any requirements to study these courses?

Our courses are suitable for anyone with the motivation to improve, upskill or reskill their screenwriting skill. All you need is a phone, tablet, laptop, or computer, and you are good to go.

How do I receive and begin my course?

Simply choose any course of your choice, click enroll now, choose a payment option and complete the one-page checkout. Once you have enrolled in any course, you will be sent a confirmation email with instructions on how to get started and access to your dashboard where your lessons will be displayed. You can start your studies right away!

Is the course interactive?

Yes! All our courses are interactive. You will learn screenwriting the “hand-on approach” way. We are constantly investing in our courses and updating the content as you learn.

How long will you have access to the online course?

WoAccelerator provides you with LIFETIME access to your course. There is no time limit for completing your courses; they can be studied in your own time at your own pace.

What student and course support do I receive?

Once you have chosen and enrolled in your course, you will get the chance to submit your script to investors and connect with other budding screenwriters.
